Mutton in Punjab, Pakistan

Mutton farming in Punjab is widespread, with sheep and goats raised in organic environments. The province produces high-quality, tender mutton that is rich in flavor and nutrients.

Health Benefits of Mutton

  • 🍖 High in zinc and iron
  • 💪 Strengthens bones and immunity
  • 🔥 Supports metabolism and energy levels

Processing & Quality Standards

Mutton in Punjab is processed under strict quality control, ensuring freshness and hygiene. Halal-certified slaughtering methods maintain its authenticity.
