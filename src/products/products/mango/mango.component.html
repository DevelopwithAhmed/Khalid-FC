Mango in Punjab, Pakistan

Mango is known as the **"King of Fruits"** and is one of the most important crops grown in Punjab, Pakistan. The region's warm climate and fertile soil create the perfect conditions for **sweet, juicy, and aromatic mangoes**.

üå± How Mangoes Grow in Punjab

In Punjab, mango cultivation begins in **February to March**, with flowering taking place as the temperature rises. The fruit matures between **May and August**, depending on the variety. Popular varieties include **Chaunsa, Sindhri, and Anwar Ratol**. Mango trees require **well-drained soil**, plenty of sunlight, and regular irrigation to thrive.
