Orange in Punjab, Pakistan

Punjab, Pakistan is famous for its **delicious, juicy, and aromatic oranges**, primarily grown in **Sargodha**.

🌱 How Oranges Grow in Punjab

Oranges in Punjab are grown in **Sargodha and Faisalabad**. The season starts in **November** and lasts until **March**.

🍊 Health Benefits of Orange

  • Boosts Immunity: High in **Vitamin C**, strengthening the immune system.
  • Good for Skin: Helps maintain **glowing and youthful skin**.
  • Heart Health: Contains **potassium**, which helps control blood pressure.
  • Improves Digestion: Rich in **fiber**, aiding in better digestion.
