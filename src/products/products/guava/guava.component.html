Guava in Punjab, Pakistan

Guava is a popular fruit grown in **Punjab, Pakistan**, known for its **sweet flavor and high nutritional value**.

🌱 How Guavas Grow in Punjab

Guavas grow best in **warm climates** with plenty of **sunlight and well-drained soil**.

🥝 Health Benefits of Guava

  • Improves Digestion: High in **fiber**, preventing constipation.
  • Boosts Heart Health: Helps control **cholesterol and blood pressure**.
  • Rich in Vitamin C: Strengthens the **immune system**.
  • Good for Diabetes: Helps regulate **blood sugar levels**.
